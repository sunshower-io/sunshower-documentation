# Topology

Topology is a visual representation of your instances: launched, pending, and terminated. Pending instances will be shown in yellow. Live instances are green, and terminated instances will be red.

# Refresh Button

If you have either launched or terminated instances, you will need to hit the refresh button in the upper right-hand corner. This will make the topology page show the changes to your instances.
